What We Do

Crafted for every stage of your gown

Whether you're starting with bare fabric or finishing a gown someone else began, our hands take it the rest of the way.

Gowns From Scratch

Full bridal and party gowns built from the first cut to the final hem — your measurements, your fabric, your vision.

Mid-Gown Work

Brought a half-stitched gown? We pick up where it left off and finish it to match the original work, seam for seam.

Marble & Stone Fixing

Fallen marbles, loose stones, sequins and mirror work — restored and re-set so the gown sits and shines like new.

Gown Designing

Not sure what you want yet? We sketch, suggest fabrics and embellishments, and shape a design around the occasion.

How It Works

From first thread to final fitting

One continuous line of work, stitched together end to end.

1

Consultation

Tell us the occasion, the gown style you have in mind, and any reference photos or fabric you've already chosen.

2

Measurements & Fabric

Precise measurements are taken in person, and we help you finalise fabric, lining and embellishment choices.

3

Design & Cutting

The design is sketched, the pattern drawn, and the fabric cut to match your shape and the gown's silhouette.

4

Stitching & Embellishment

The gown is hand and machine stitched, with marbles, stones, sequins or mirror work added as designed.

5

Fitting

A trial fitting checks length, drape and comfort, with adjustments made on the spot where needed.

6

Final Delivery

The finished gown is pressed, checked stone by stone, and handed over ready to wear.

About Lovely Tailors

Led by hand, run by Elango

Lovely Tailors is a small, dedicated tailoring shop built around one craft: gowns. Every piece that leaves the shop has passed through Elango's own hands — from the first measurement to the last stone set in place.

  • Personal attention on every gown — no piece leaves without a final check.
  • Comfortable taking over gowns that another tailor left midway.
  • Careful, stone-by-stone repair work on embellished and bridal gowns.
  • Honest guidance on design, fabric and fit — what suits the gown, and what suits you.
